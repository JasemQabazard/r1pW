About us

I launched the first site for this application on 2010, as Read1Page.com. This release expands the application to further generate recital codes so that you can share a recital with your friends or your family when you wish to do so. The idea is meant to be simple while secure, hence a compromise was made to make the recital code short and not have user register or sign on. We use your email id to send you the recital code you generate via an email letter once, we do not store your email id or use it for any other purpose. The general reecital codes are the default for everyone to share a global recital of the Holly Quran. Please include your brother moslims in your prayers.
