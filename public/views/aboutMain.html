About us

I launched the first site for this application on 2010, as Read1Page.com. This release expands the application to further generate recital codes so that you can share a reciotyal with your friends or your family when you wish to do so. The idea is meant to be simple while secure, hence a compromise was made to make the recital code short and not have user sign on. The email entry is there to send you the recital code you generate via email once, we are not storing your email or using it for any other purpose. The general reecital code is the default for everyone to share a global recital of the Holly Quran. Please include your brother moslims in your prayers.
